The problem

Habit tracking shouldn't feel like admin work.

You already know what you want to do. The friction isn't motivation — it's that most apps treat progress like spreadsheet rows.

01

Functional, not delightful

Checkmarks and number counters work, but they don't reward a glance. Nothing on your home screen makes you want to log the next rep.

02

Widgets that whisper "utility"

Lock Screen and Home Screen real estate is precious. Generic list widgets look like reminders — not like progress you're proud of.

03

One visual language for everything

Running, reading, hydration, meditation — they aren't the same habit. Why should they all look identical?

Meridian treats each habit as its own small world — the shape fills as you show up.

Built for daily use

Deep enough to trust. Simple enough to tap.

Meridian is a native iOS app with interactive widgets, streak tracking, and a completion history view — all running locally on your device.

◉

Interactive widgets — log from the Lock Screen

Five widget families across Lock Screen and Home Screen. Tap the metaphor to log a rep; tap the text to open streak detail. Home Screen widgets support a configurable habit picker.

Streak detail

Hero-sized metaphor, 7-day completion strip, current and longest streak, total completions, and ± step controls when you need to adjust.

◎

Daily & weekly cadence

Each habit resets on its own schedule — every day, or every week with a configurable week start. Counts never carry over from a previous period.

▦

View tab

A GitHub-style completion grid per habit — weekdays as rows, weeks as columns. See patterns at a glance without leaving the app.

☰

Hold to reorder

Drag habits into the order that matches your morning. Add and edit with a live preview card — what you type is what you get.

Privacy by design

Local-first. Optional cloud backup.

No account required. Habits stay on your iPhone with SwiftData. Sign in with Apple or Google only if you want backup across devices — powered by Supabase, with no analytics SDKs.
