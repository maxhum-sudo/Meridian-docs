Meridian Support

Help with the Meridian habit tracker for iOS.

Report a bug or ask a question

Open a GitHub Issue — include iOS version, Meridian version (Settings → About), and steps to reproduce.

Quick answers

  • Widgets show no habits? Open Meridian and add at least one habit, then add the widget from the iOS Home Screen or Lock Screen widget gallery.
  • Widget tap doesn’t update? Ensure the widget is a Meridian interactive widget (tap the metaphor to log a rep). Reload widgets after large data changes.
  • Weekly habits — week boundaries follow Settings → Week starts on (locale, Sunday, or Monday).
  • Data location — all habits stay on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
  • After an app update — if habits disappear, Meridian may have moved your old database to a StoreBackups/ folder on device; contact us via GitHub Issues for recovery help.
